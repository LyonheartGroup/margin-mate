How MarginMate thinks

  • Delivery cost = hours × internal cost/hour
  • Revision cost = delivery cost × revision buffer

  • Recommended quote = total real cost ÷ (1 - target margin)

FOUNDER NOTE

I built this because pricing services is where many consultants quietly lose.

When I started consulting, I had no clear idea what to charge. I did not want to scare good clients away with pricing that felt too high, but I also refused to build myself a worse job by undercharging.

The hardest part was not just picking a number. It was separating services clearly enough that one client buying one thing did not assume they had purchased the entire suite. Over time, I built a more systematic way to price around two realities: the value created in the client’s mind and the hours required to deliver the work properly.

That approach helped me grow a profitable consulting business, hire people, and deliver work clients still feel is worth the price. MarginMate is the distilled version of that thinking: clearer scope, cleaner pricing, better margins.
