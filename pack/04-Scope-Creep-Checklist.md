# Scope Creep Checklist

Use this before the proposal is sent and again before work begins.

## Scope definition
- What exactly is included?
- What is explicitly excluded?
- How many deliverables are included?
- How many revision rounds are included?
- What counts as a change request?

## Timeline control
- What are the delivery milestones?
- What client inputs are required to stay on schedule?
- What happens if feedback is late?
- Is there a rush fee if deadlines compress?

## Pricing protection
- Is the quoted price based on a fixed scope?
- Are extra rounds billed separately?
- Are new requests billed hourly or per deliverable?
- Is there a kill fee or minimum commitment?

## Communication control
- Who is the single client point of contact?
- What feedback format is required?
- What happens if feedback is fragmented or contradictory?

## Approval checkpoints
- What needs formal approval before the next phase?
- When does work count as accepted?
- What happens if approval is delayed?

## Useful line
"Happy to do that. It falls outside the current scope, so I’ll price it as an additional change request."
