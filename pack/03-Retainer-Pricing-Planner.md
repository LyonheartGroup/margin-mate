## Sanity checks
- Is the scope fixed enough to price cleanly?
- Is this really a retainer or just a disguised project?
- Will the client expect unlimited access?
- Are approval delays likely to create hidden work?

## Decision note
If the retainer is vague, the margin is imaginary.
